Total Dead

We attempted to use linear regression to try and predict the number of total dead and missing. In order to try and generate a better prediction, we focused on the total dead and missing by month and then coded each month into one of four seasons. Based on the training and test scores, we determined that we were unable to predicted the total number of dead and missing.

However, when we tried to predict the total number of men, women, and children dead or missing, the scores improved. The best training and test scores were attributed to men, which we believe is based on the fact that the number of men reported to be dead and missing within the dataset is more robust and possibly tracked more accurately.
